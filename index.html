Have a question? Check out the FAQ

We have answered some of the most common questions to help you understand how we work and serve you better.

What service does ISFECOM offer?

We handele customs clearance, freight forwarding, import/export documentation, and cargo tracking.

How long does custom clearance take in Nigeria?

Depending on documention and item category, it can take from 2 to 5 working days.

What documents do I need to clear goods?

Basic documents include Form M, Bill of Lading, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, and SONCAP (if required).

Can you help with international shipping arrangements?

Yes, we worl with trusted global logistics partners to arrange smooth overseas shipping.

Do you offer container tracking?

Absolutely. Clients are provides with real-time tracking updates for transparency.

Is there insurance for goods in transit?

Yes, we offer cargo insurance options to secure your shipment from pickup to delivery.
